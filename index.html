Project notes

Architecture highlights

  • Frontend: vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for an easy-to-run MVP.
  • Storage: browser localStorage for check-ins, moods, journal entries, and habits.
  • AI simulation: rule-based empathy engine, emotion scoring, and coping recommendations.
  • Safety: crisis phrase detection, non-diagnostic language, and urgent support redirection.

This demo supports reflection and habit building, but it does not replace a licensed mental health professional or emergency care.
